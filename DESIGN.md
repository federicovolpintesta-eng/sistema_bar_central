# Design System: Bar Central - Control de Inventario Premium

## Visual Identity & Theme
- **Theme**: Premium Dark Mode (Luxurious Hotel Bar & Nightclub aesthetic).
- **Primary Color**: Vibrant Cyan (#06B6D4) and Emerald Green (#10B981) for active stock.
- **Accent Color**: Elegant Gold/Amber (#F59E0B) for alerts and warnings. Crimson (#EF4444) for critical stock or breakages.
- **Backgrounds**: Deep Obsidian (#0B0F19) and Slate (#1E293B) for container cards with subtle glassmorphism (border-white/10).

## Typography
- **Font Family**: 'Inter', sans-serif. Modern, crisp, and clean.
- **Metrics & Numbers**: High contrast, bold tabular numbers for stock levels, minimal alerts, and percentage gauges.

## Component Styling
- **Cards**: Rounded corners (16px / rounded-2xl), dark translucent backgrounds with backdrop blur, subtle glow on hover.
- **Buttons**: Smooth hover state transitions, vibrant gradients for primary CTAs like "Registrar Movimiento" and "Exportar a Google Sheets".
- **Status Badges**: Pill badges with clear iconography: Normal (✅ Verde), Alerta Crítica (⚠️ Ambar/Rojo), Merma (📉 Rojo).
- **Tables / Grids**: Clean padded rows, sticky headers, quick filter buttons by category (Bebidas con Alcohol, Cervezas, Sin Alcohol, Cafetería, Vajilla).
